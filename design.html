App

Subject: Screen of bar graph on reading app
Target: Men in their twenties who tend to pile unread books but want to enjoy reading
The point of this design is the colour that looks like library.
By using colours that make people remind of historical libraries, they can feel like they're in a library wherever they are and it makes them read more books.
On the top, you can choose from "Day, week, month, year" and bar graph will change.
The current screen tells you time sideways and pages lengthwise.
Then, on the bottom, graphs are placed to compare today with yesterday so that they can be motivated.

Subject: Screen of calender
Target: women in thier twenties who aim to be independent
The points of this design is that they can write a message to themselves or goals in the month on the top.
People open this app every day because they usually check their schedules for the day, so through memorization to see the message, they can act actually and improve themselves.
So, I placed space for detailed schedules including time and content.
It appear a circle on more important schedules on the list to manage your days efficiently.
Improvement is the colour.
I used too childish bright pink, but light pink or orange would be better for mature women.

Subject: Search screen of map app
Target: Junior high school students who just get their first smart phone
As a good point, I put space to write necessary information on a place everyone can reach.
Especially, space of date and departure are easy to use when they're in a hurry.
Also, the design is simple, so they can use whenever, whoever and when growing up.
